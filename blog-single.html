Trip to Sta. Ana, Cagayan

A Hidden Gem in the Northern Philippines
Scroll

Introduction

Tucked away in the northernmost reaches of Luzon, the municipality of Sta. Ana in Cagayan province is a hidden gem that promises a unique blend of natural beauty, rich culture, and thrilling adventures. Often overshadowed by more popular tourist destinations in the Philippines, Sta. Ana offers an unspoiled paradise for those willing to take the road less traveled.

Getting There!

Reaching Sta. Ana, Cagayan requires a bit of effort but is well worth it. From Manila, you can take a flight to Tuguegarao City, the capital of Cagayan. From Tuguegarao, Sta. Ana is about a 3-4 hour drive through scenic landscapes and quaint towns. Alternatively, for the more adventurous, there are long-distance buses that travel directly from Manila to Sta. Ana, offering a more economical and immersive journey.

Where to stay?

Sta. Ana offers a range of accommodations to suit different budgets and preferences. From luxurious resorts like the Cagayan Holiday and Leisure Resort, which boasts a casino and a golf course, to more budget-friendly options such as beachfront cottages and local inns, visitors will find comfortable lodging options. For a more authentic experience, consider staying in homestays offered by the friendly locals.

Attractions

1. Palaui Island

Arguably the crown jewel of Sta. Ana, Palaui Island is a protected landscape and seascape offering pristine beaches, rugged cliffs, and rich biodiversity. A short boat ride from the mainland, this island is perfect for hiking, snorkeling, and simply basking in the natural beauty. The trek to Cape Enga√±o Lighthouse rewards adventurers with panoramic views of the island and the surrounding waters.

2. Anguib Beach

Known for its powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters, Anguib Beach is often compared to the famous Boracay, but without the crowds. This secluded beach is ideal for swimming, sunbathing, and enjoying a peaceful day by the sea. There are facilities for picnicking and camping, making it a great spot for a family outing.
